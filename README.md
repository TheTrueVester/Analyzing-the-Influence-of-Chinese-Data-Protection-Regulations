# Analyzing the Influence of Chinese Data Protection Regulations on Foreign Companies Operating in China

This repository contains the empirical materials and publications for the research I did with Vinicius Agreste with the guidance of Mo Chen and Prof. Jens Großklags on how foreign companies operating in China adapt to **Chinese data protection regulations**, with a particular focus on **PIPL, DSL, and CSL**.

## Contents

- **Thesis_Final_Version.pdf**  
  Full thesis detailing the theoretical background, research design, methodology, and findings.

- **ECIS-25_camera ready.pdf**  
  Conference paper presented at the *European Conference on Information Systems (ECIS) 2025*:  
  *“Adapting to Coercive Forces: How Foreign Companies Respond to China’s Data Protection Regulations.”*

- **Coding File.xlsx**  
  Qualitative coding schema and coded interview data (anonymized).

- **Interviewed Companies Overview Sheet.pdf**  
  Overview of interviewed organizations and key characteristics (anonymized).
